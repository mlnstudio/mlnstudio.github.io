Our Vision: True Freedom in Computing

MLN Studio is an open-source group and studio operating under the GPLv3 license, emphasizing the principles of freedom, security, and universal participation in software development. We believe the future of computing should be in the hands of the community, not large corporations.

We are dissatisfied with the current direction of existing operating systems, which tend towards monopolization and restriction. Our goal is to build a more secure and libre alternative; a system where anyone can contribute, and no one is rejected without cause.

Freedom

We believe in software freedom and everyone's right to use, study, modify, and distribute it. MLN OS is the embodiment of this freedom.

Security

Our commitment is to build an operating system with the highest security standards, ensuring users can operate with peace of mind.

Community

MLN Studio is built on collective participation. We believe in the power of collaboration and rejecting no programmer.

Innovation

We seek new and creative solutions in kernel and operating system development to build a better future.

MLN OS

MLN OS is our flagship operating system, meticulously crafted for the modern era of computing. It's built on the principles of ultimate freedom, robust security, and a truly community-driven development model. We aim to provide an unparalleled user experience that respects your privacy and gives you full control over your digital life.

Unlike other systems, MLN OS is not influenced by corporate agendas. It's developed by a passionate global community, ensuring it remains libre and secure for everyone.

MLN Rooms

MLN Rooms is a secure and decentralized communication platform designed to foster collaboration and community among MLN OS users and contributors. We prioritize your privacy and ensure your conversations remain secure and free from surveillance.

Built with end-to-end encryption and a focus on user control, MLN Rooms provides a safe space for discussions, development coordination, and general interaction within the MLN Studio ecosystem.

Nermux Emulator

Nermux is a lightweight yet powerful emulator designed specifically for testing and developing on the Nerkel Kernel and MLN OS. It provides a stable and consistent environment for developers to experiment with new features, debug code, and explore the system's capabilities without needing dedicated hardware.

Currently supporting about 10 commands and a simple boot process, Nermux is constantly evolving, making it an essential tool for anyone looking to dive into MLN OS development.

Download Nermux (Coming Soon) Contribute to Nermux

Morningglory WM

Morningglory WM is a highly customizable and efficient window manager built from the ground up for MLN OS. Designed for speed and flexibility, it offers a minimalist yet powerful interface that allows users to tailor their desktop experience precisely to their needs.

With a focus on resource efficiency and user freedom, Morningglory WM embodies the MLN Studio philosophy, providing a truly libre and adaptable desktop environment.
